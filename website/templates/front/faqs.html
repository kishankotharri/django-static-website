Frequently Asked Questions

1. What does the main screen display after logging in?

The main screen shows the schedule for today and the next seven days. If there are no appointments, it will display an empty home page with dates.

2.How do I create a new appointment?

Click on the 'Create' button on the main screen, then select from the four options: Quotation, Installation, Service, or Other, and fill in the required details.

3. What happens if I forget to fill in mandatory fields while creating an appointment?

If you attempt to submit the form with any mandatory fields left empty, an error message will appear stating, “Please fill in all required fields.”

4.Can I edit profile, site, or scheduling details after creating an appointment?

Yes, you can click the edit button on the confirmation page to redirect to the respective profile, site, or scheduling details page for any updates.

5.Where can I find all the products?

You can access all products from the Product List section in the app. Simply click on the Product List page to view all available products and their categories. You can also filter products by category or use the search bar to find specific items.

6. How do I view my quotation or invoice history?

You can view your last 3 or 6 months' quotations and invoices by clicking on the respective options in the history section. You can also apply custom date filters to see specific records.
