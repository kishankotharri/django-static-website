Privacy Policy

Last Modified: Nov 13, 2024

Welcome to TaskTracker Pro! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Tech Innovators (“we,” “us,” “our”) collects, uses, shares, and protects information related to the TaskTracker Pro app (“app” or “service”). By using TaskTracker Pro, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you use TaskTracker Pro, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Account Information: Full name, email address, phone number, and company name.
  • Billing Information: Payment details for service subscriptions, which are processed securely through third-party payment processors.

Non-Personal Information

We also collect non-personal information such as:

  • Usage Data: App interactions, preferences, and feature usage.
  • Device Information: Information about your device, such as model, operating system, IP address, and unique device identifiers.

Location Information

If you enable location services, we may collect location data to enhance scheduling, route planning, and location-based service requests.

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and Improve Services: Operate, maintain, and enhance TaskTracker Pro’s features for efficient business management.
  • Communicate with You: Send updates, alerts, and information related to your account, including support and billing notifications.
  • Analyze Usage: Understand usage trends to improve app functionality, optimize features, and make the user experience more efficient.
  • Personalize Content: Customize the app to suit your preferences and improve user satisfaction.

3. How We Share Information

Third-Party Service Providers

We may share data with third-party vendors who provide services such as payment processing, data storage, analytics, and customer support.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or in response to legal requests, or to protect the rights, property, and safety of our company, users, or others.

Business Transfers

If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will notify you of any such change.

4. Security of Your Information

We implement industry-standard security measures, including data encryption, secure storage, and access controls, to protect your information. However, please note that no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

5. Your Choices and Rights

Access and Update

You may access and update your account information through the app. Please ensure your information is accurate to avoid service issues.

Data Deletion

To delete your account or personal data, please contact us at support@tasktrackerpro.com. We will process your request promptly, though certain data may be retained for legal or operational purposes.

Marketing Preferences

You can opt-out of marketing emails by following the unsubscribe link in any marketing communication. Note that we may still send transactional or account-related emails.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve app performance and user experience. You can control cookies through your device settings, but this may affect certain app features.

7. Children’s Privacy

TaskTracker Pro is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 16. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 16 without parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes via email or an in-app notification. The date of the latest update is indicated at the top of this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Tech Innovators
Email: support@tasktrackerpro.com

Thank you for using TaskTracker Pro. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a secure experience.
