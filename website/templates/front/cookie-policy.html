Cookie Policy

Last Modified: Nov 13, 2024

This Cookie Policy explains how TaskTracker Pro ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve user experience, enhance functionality, and analyze usage patterns.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you access our app. They help us remember your preferences and improve your experience with TaskTracker Pro.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Recognize and remember your login status and preferences.
  • Analyze usage to optimize app performance.

Your Choices

You can control cookies through your browser or device settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain features of the app.

Contact Us

If you have questions about our Cookie Policy, contact us at support@tasktrackerpro.com.
